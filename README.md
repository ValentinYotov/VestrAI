# VestrAI

VestrAI is an AI-powered mobile application designed to provide real-time investment recommendations across cryptocurrencies, stocks, and forex. Users can input their investment preferences (budget, risk tolerance, and investment horizon), and the app's AI will analyze market data to suggest the most suitable investment options. The app is built to be cross-platform, working seamlessly on both iOS and Android, with a focus on user-friendly interfaces, smart analytics, and personalized investment strategies.
